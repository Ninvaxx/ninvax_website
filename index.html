About Me

I'm Christopher “Ninvax” Moore—a cybersecurity specialist, nuclear Navy veteran and data analyst based in River Edge, NJ. My career blends systems engineering with a curiosity for AI and simulation.

The Navy labeled me high-scoring and nuclear qualified, but I chose to step back and rethink how the system works. Now I build, break and rebuild: exploring cybersecurity, data engineering and systems design to protect what matters.

I study how rules are written, who benefits from them, and how they can be improved. Whether creating an app, an exploit or a workflow, I design for edge cases—because I am one.
